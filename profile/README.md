#  World Bank ECA Transport Unit

> A  knowledge hub for GIS and spatial data workflows supporting transport infrastructure projects across the **Europe & Central Asia** region.

---

## Who We Are

We are the **Transport Unit of the World Bank's Europe & Central Asia (ECA)** regional practice. This GitHub organisation is a space where our team **documents, curates, and shares** spatial workflows, GIS resources, and transport data pipelines  making our methods transparent, reproducible, and accessible to the wider community.

---

## What We Aim to Share

We are actively building out this space with the following resources:

- **Spatial data workflows** - sourcing, cleaning, and processing geospatial datasets for transport applications
- **GIS pipelines** - tools and scripts for preparing and managing spatial data across different zone systems and formats
- **GIS knowledge base** - guides and documentation on spatial concepts and methods, written for transport specialists
- **Transport spatial analytics** - resources supporting spatial analysis and data-driven transport decision making

---

## Our Approach

We do not host raw data. The datasets referenced across our repositories are sourced from publicly available platforms, national statistics portals, and international organisations. What we share here is the **code, documentation, and workflows** needed to access, process, and use them effectively.

All code is shared under the **MIT License** unless otherwise stated in individual repositories.
